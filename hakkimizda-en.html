— Our Story

The Story Behind the Flavour

Since 2013, EAGLE Chinese Gastro & Sushi has been serving sushi, wok dishes and Far Eastern flavors in a modern and welcoming restaurant atmosphere in Sisli Bomonti. Guests can enjoy the EAGLE experience through dine-in, delivery and pick-up.

We keep familiar Far Eastern techniques at the center while serving them in a warm, contemporary restaurant setting for guests of every age.

Our menu brings together sushi, sashimi, wok dishes, spring rolls, dim sum and a broad selection of Far Eastern favorites.
